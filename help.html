Help - SimDra Drawing Game

Welcome to SimDra!

SimDra is a simple and enjoyable drawing game that allows you to unleash your creativity on a digital canvas. Here are some tips to help you get started:

Instructions:

  1. Click and hold on the canvas to start drawing.
  2. Move your mouse or finger to create your artwork.
  3. Release to stop drawing.

Features:

  • The color changes automatically every second, adding variety to your drawings.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different colors to create vibrant drawings.
  • Let your imagination run wild and draw anything you can think of!
