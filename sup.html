Ways to Support Us

SimDra is a passion project, and your support means a lot to us! Here are some ways you can contribute and help us improve and grow:

1. Spread the Word

Share SimDra with your friends, family, and on social media. The more people know about the game, the better!

2. Provide Feedback

We value your feedback. Let us know about your experience, suggest improvements, or report any issues you encounter.

3. Donate

Consider making a donation to support the development and maintenance of SimDra. Every contribution, big or small, helps.

4. Get Involved

If you're a developer, designer, or enthusiast, consider contributing to the project. Check out our GitHub repository for more information.
