SimDra - Game Info

Welcome to SimDra!

SimDra is a simple and enjoyable drawing game designed to unleash your creativity. Here's more information about the game:

Features:

  • Easy-to-use drawing interface.
  • Automatic color changes every second for added variety.
  • Responsive design for both mobile and desktop.
  • Customizable options to enhance your drawing experience.

Instructions:

  1. Click and hold on the canvas to start drawing.
  2. Move your mouse or finger to create your artwork.
  3. Release to stop drawing.

Customization Options:

  • Click the "Customize" button to access various drawing settings.
  • Explore different colors for diverse artistic creations.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different colors to create vibrant drawings.
  • Let your imagination run wild!
