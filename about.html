About Simdra Drawing Game

Welcome to Simdra drawing Game!

Simple Drawing Game offers a creative and relaxing experience, allowing you to unleash your artistic talents through a straightforward drawing interface.

Features

  • Intuitive drawing canvas
  • Change colors every second for added variety
  • Compatible with both desktop and mobile devices

How to Play

To start drawing, click and drag on the canvas. You can change colors by tapping the screen (on mobile) or clicking the mouse (on desktop). The color changes every second, adding a dynamic element to your artwork.
